Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, widely known as Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was a visionary leader, educator, and scientist, often referred to as the "Missile Man of India" for his role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. Dr. Kalam dedicated his life to science and technology, and he inspired millions with his words and work.
